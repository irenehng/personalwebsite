Why Crochet?

1. creativity: It's amazing what you can create with a hook and a ball of yarn. Each piece of clothing is like wearable art!
2. slow fashion: slow down to appreciate the craftsmanship and raw materials that go into clothing production
3. spread joy: fashion is a tool for self expression and I want to make that more accessible to everyone. Everyone deserves garments that bring them joy, fits well, and is ethically made, just for them.

I love picking up hobbies and using my skills to contribute to my community. A portion of the profits I made from my garments goes towards supporting a social cause!
